In preparing this message I realized something very important. The reason we worship God is to equip us to receive the fruits of the spirit.  
If we are not in a position of praise and worship, we can not receive the fruits of the spirit from God.  
  

### Galatians 5:22-23

New King James Version (NKJV)

22 But the fruit of the Spirit is love, joy, peace, longsuffering, kindness, goodness, faithfulness, 23 gentleness, self-control. Against such there is no law.  
  
If we are not in a perpetual place of worship, honest true worship of God, we can not receive His love. Joy will be lost to us and peace will be impossible to find.  
Without purposeful worship we will not be able to suffershort let alone have longsuffering, kindness will be for our own end and not for the sake of kindness goodness? it will simply not exist as we will be full of ourselves instead.  
Faithfulness? why would we need to be faithful if we aren't actually faithful ourselves. Gentleness and self control will simply fall by the wayside.  
  
As we pursue our own goals and wishes ignoring the things of God.  Hence we must worship. Worship brings our hearts to a place before the sovereign God of Heaven, a place of abject submission. We submit ourselves to him in adoration, worshipful devotion and then we can be prepared to receive the fruits of the Spirit. Obviously, once we have a fruit, we need to pass it on, share it with others and respond to God in further praise and adulation.  
  
Worship can not be planned by a committee. Planned worship becomes something else, something foreign to God and not becoming of His true worthiness. In fact planned worship can become an idol taking all of the praise and worship meant for God for itself.  We must be certain that we worship the God of heaven because we are full of worship, and not because someone tricks us into doing so.
