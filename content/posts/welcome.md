Greetings,  
I have been feeling the urge, desire, or direction to start blogging about the things we learn in church each Sunday. I will be linking to the audio for each service.  (I'll post links to the existing service recordings) and going forward discussing in greater (?) detail the things that I learn and see in each of the messages.  
  
First a little word about who we are. Greer Church Without Walls is a small home church, our vision isn't to have a congregation of thousands, or hundreds, or even dozens. Out goal is to life the name of Jesus so that "all men will be drawn" to Him.  
  
If we have submitted to this for only one person, then it is worth it. Jesus would have died for one person to be saved, how much easier is it for me to lay down the preconceived ideas of what a church is in order to reach out to one or two or a dozen young people who otherwise, might never have any insight to the things of God.  
  
We Moved to South Carolina in response to an inner prompting of the Holy Spirit, That I was called to support the ministry of Rev. Thomas Young, as he had supported the ministry of Rev. Loran W. Helm throughout the years. We moved her and became a part of the Travelers Rest Christ Fellowship, in Travelers Rest, SC ([TRCF Web site](http://www.christfellowship.net/Christ_Fellowship/Welcome.html)) They are a group of loving people who welcomed us into their family. In the year 2001 God began pushing me toward my calling, the calling to be a minister of the wonderful and marvelous word of God. When I was 13 Rev. Helm had looked at me, while at our home and said, "You are called to preach the glorious wonders, and riches of the word of God. But it may not be in a church, or a sunday school."  (That is a paraphrase, I was 13... it was a long time ago) I still remember clearly the move of the Holy Spirit in me witnessing to what was said. In I John it says there are three witnesses on Earth, The Spirt, the water and the blood, (The spirit as breathed into man, the flesh (95%+ water) and the Blood (The soul, the very WHO I am) The Holy Spirit witnessed within me to the rightness of that calling. I spent many years trying to avoid it or only touch the edges...  
  
In 2001 I was thrust head on into that calling. We had lost almost everything due to poor financial planning on my part, and the loss of an excellent job to over seas competition. I was bankrupt, and desperate, yet still proud and thinking too highly of myself. God had a lot of work, and still does, to get me to a point where I could trust Him.  
  
After the calling came again, in the middle of Service Rev. Thomas Young felt the direction to pray a prayer of ordination over me... it just happened. nothing changed in who I was but something changed in how I felt.  I accepted that mantle of Ministry, with care, and we started down a long road of God blocking things that were our own "good ideas"  
  
At first we thought there would be a church, and we would start in a small building and have services etc. etc..  then we though maybe a daycare, to pay for the church... then there was thoughts of.. lots of things. Eventually in 2010 God moved in me that it was time to either Do what He wanted me to do (which was step out and simply follow him without planning, or even thinking, only following) or to stay where we were and lose the anointing of the Holy Spirit, and perhaps even cause the church to be in a dry place due to the "sin" of inaction, of not trusting, of not following.  
  
We left, it wasn't easy, not everyone understood, I think some still don't, but we love all the people there the same and I am always happy to see any of them.  
  
We in June of 2010 we started having a service in our living room on Sunday mornings. There were a couple of families who came and God helped us. Over time people stopped coming for various reasons and others started coming. young people who are friends of our children started coming and taking part. These are the ones that we are called to, these young people who have lives that aren't perfect. We open our home to them and love them, offering them a place to come and rest, to sleep, to find peace.  
  
Peace that "passes understanding" Peace that lets them come and just feel safe and secure.  If one of these young people find their place in God's will then it is worth the whole.  
  
We started putting messages on the internet, I felt that was what God wanted... I started with video.. not because I like video of myself but the site I found was setup that way... Since then I have found a site where I can broadcast live "radio" and record it for later listening too.  
  
As of present, there have been 1,314 plays of 55 messages. I don't know who listens to them I simply pray that God will speak to their hearts and that Jesus will be lifted up in their lives as well.  
  
I started a series,almost by accident, with a message [A is for Abba](http://www.spreaker.com/user/4882699/a_is_for_abba)  and have worked our way to V as of today. (Hint, V is for Victory)  
I will post an article with links to the saved messages in case anyone wants to listen to them.  
  
I trust and Pray that I will be able to keep this Blog and update it with the things that need to be updated.  
  
Jonathan Wagner  
Pastor
