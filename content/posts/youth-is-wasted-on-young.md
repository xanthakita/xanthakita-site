## Youth is Wasted on the Young

  
At least that's what they say. Young people go too fast, pay too little attention and miss things that older people would enjoy...  
  
HA  
  
All we are doing is making excuses for ourselves. I used to be a young person, and I still feel young, but the calendar tells me I am older.   Also the sobering things that happen, such as three teens losing control of their car and wrapping around a tree in a fiery crash less than 100 yards from my home. Two young men died there and one was taken to the burn center, I don't know what his status is, except I know he needs prayer.  
  
I realized that it doesn't matter why the accident happened. It doesn't matter who was doing what.  All that matters is did someone lift the name of Jesus so those young men had a chance to be drawn to Him?  
  
Jesus said, "If I be lifted up, I'll draw all men to me."  
  
Did we lift Jesus up so that these young men could see him? Did we lift the name of Jesus high enough? I don't know the answer to that, and probably won't till I stand at judgement.  
  
You see there are many things that I don't think God judges us on.. I don't think God cares how we dress we care... a lot and modesty is really appreciated! but God made Adam and Eve naked he only clothed them after they learned they were naked...  
  
I don't think God judges us on how we eat, what we drink, if we smoke or not. I think, ok I know, that God will judge us on how we interact with the Holy Spirit and our relationship with His Son.  
  
That's it, pretty simple, huh. We get all wrapped up in things that were meant to make life easier for us, the laws of the old testament, the moral certitudes of the present day, and I am pretty sure that God isn't the least bit concerned. I am certain that God wept that morning, as those young men suffered, I hope not much. He was there drawing them to Himself, I can only hope they had some relationship with Him and with His son.  
  
We can stop getting all upset over what God meant in the Old Testament or what God meant in the New Testament.  What is or isn't an abomination. God told us that the only unforgivable sin is to blaspheme the Holy Spirit... I don't want to do that.. I'm not even really sure what that means.  
  
Now I don't mean to say that we shouldn't "be good" that we should go out and sin our brains out.. not at all. The Holy Spirit will impress on you what you should and shouldn't do. Our spirit will talk to us and we will simply KNOW if we are about to do or are doing something that God isn't in. If you find yourself in that position.. don't do whatever it is!  Have some common sense!  
  
Remember that God loves you... God is Love Love never ceases, it doesn't make boasts about itself and it's not all egotistical. God is THAT love! Just remember that.  
  
The next time you see young people going too fast or acting too bravely (stupidly) pray for them, try to have a conversation with them, not about what they were doing, but about their lives. you might just be surprised. what you learn.
