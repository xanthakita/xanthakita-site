Choices, wow they can have such long lasting effects on our lives. Often the choices that we make which have the most impact, seem the least significant at the time.  
  
Choosing to room with someone may seem like a minor thing, but it has the potential to totally destroy your entire life.  Choosing to drive one road instead of another may be minor, but might be the choice that saves you from a terrible accident. Choosing to do that one last thing, might just make you late enough to miss one of life's key moments.  
  
My point is that the Holy Spirit will guide us, if we listen. If we listen to that small still voice, sometimes we have to really struggle to be quite enough to hear. The Holy Spirit will help us hear what He wants us do to.  God never forces us, but He will show us. We don't have to blindly stumble through life, use a guide that won't ever leave you stranded with no coverage. The Holy Spirit will never leave us, If we remain true and faithful, then we can hear what the holy Spirit is whispering to us. We can feel the impressions of Gods fingers on our heart and make the right choices.  
  
My time is up,  
My peace I give to you
