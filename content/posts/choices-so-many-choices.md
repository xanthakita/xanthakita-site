Did you ever notice that today we have so many choices. It seems like as time has gone on, we, as human beings, have gotten more and more choices. From big things like where to live to little things like what sort of gum to buy.  
  
There are so many choices, and yet we don't seem happy. Have you noticed that the more choices we have, the more stressed out we seem to be. Kids get stressed trying to decide what toys to play with. If you only have one toy, then there is no stress in choosing. You either play with the toy, or not.  
  
As a developer there are dozens of ways to accomplish the same thing, do I use the "right" method, the trendy method, the fast method, or the best method?  And, not to mention, who chooses what is "best"?  
  
Well my time is up.  
  
My peace I give to you,  
Jonathan
